2.4 Project 4

Working title: Patch dynamics and regeneration in an estuarine mangrove forest due to upland shifts in hydrology

Overview: This study will examine the influence of a hydroelectric dam built upland of the mangrove forest in Pak Panang, Thailand. There are patches of mangrove die-off, which locals in the forest attribute to the construction of a hydroelectric dam several years prior. I am interested in monitoring the gap dynamics (through RS work), regeneration dynamics within these patches, as well as the emissions of soil C due to the loss of vegetation cover.
